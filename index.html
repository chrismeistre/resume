I love breaking things and providing solutions on how to secure them

I have been in the IT industry since 1998, and have spent 18 of those years as a professional software developer. I have always had a hacker mentality and officially made the transition to Cyber Security in 2020.

I obtained my OSCP, OSEP, OSWE, OSED, and OSCE3 certifications from Offensive Security within a year of starting to study.

At Checkmark Cyber I worked as a Red Teamer and Penetration Tester on a vast number of projects, including international big brand clients. I successfully performed a number of blackbox, whitebox and social engineer simluations against clients.

During my time at Halborn, I was exposed to various Web 3 technologies (smart contracts, blockchain, and DeFI) and was promoted to Lead Offensive Security Engineer within the first three (3) of starting at the company. I was tasked with performing penetration testing on web applications and cloud infrastructure. I was also responsible for performing code audits on web and desktop applications, as well as browser extensions.

I am passionate about hacking and love learning daily. The more difficult the challenge, the more satisfying it is when the solution is found. I take pride in providing work that is of the highest quality.

I know how to work well in a team, and can operate independently as well. The majority of the work that I am involved with is remote, so I can be disciplined enough to stick to deadlines.
